# System Help

### Operation and Design Principles

In the design of this site, we have attempted to strike a balance between

visually appealing graphic content and low-bandwidth speed. We encourage the

reader to keep on inline graphics loading to fully appreciate this site. We

provide information about the size of the graphics you are downloading beside

the image itself if it is more than 20k in size, otherwise the size of each

graphic and the total for the page is listed at the very bottom of each page.

And in an effort to make this site easily navigable, we have attempted to

provide links that take you "up" to relevant higher-level documents. Where

possible, these links will be found in the series of heads and subheads at the

top of each page.

Further, the upper-leftmost icon on every page will take you to the ANIMA home

page, and the icon immediately next to it will take you to the top page of a

given section.
