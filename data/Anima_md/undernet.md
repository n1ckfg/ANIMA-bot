# [Infomy. undernet.*](infomy.html)

## December 31, 1994

As one of the sponsors of INFOMY, undernet.* (an international Vancouver based

company) is actively testing consumer reaction to the options available to them

as a result of new  developments in telecommunications.  Fibre optic technology

is changing the world as we know it, with more options such as video

conferencing, interactive programming and others, more possibilities for new

markets continue to present themselves.

With a membership of over 2000 people and a growth rate of over 70% per month,

undernet.* has clearly proven a consumer interest in creating a new

entertainment format based on communication, using existing technology to tap

into a new consumer base.

Bringing the world together is not a new idea, but now for the first time

international communication is accesible.  This is what undernet.* hopes to do

with INFOMY, by bringing BC together to talk to the world.
