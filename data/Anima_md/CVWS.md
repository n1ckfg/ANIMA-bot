# Canadian Virtual Worlds Society

## Information

#### Canadian Virtual Worlds Society

Derek Dowden, President / Weston Triemstra, Chair

The Canadian Virtual Worlds Society is a not-for-profit special interest group

founded in 1992 focusing on artistic, cultural and philosophic implications of

new media and emerging technologies such as telecommunications and virtual reality.
