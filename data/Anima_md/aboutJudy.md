## Judy Malloy

<!-- 🖼️❌ Image not available. Please use `PdfPipelineOptions(generate_picture_images=True)` -->

Judy Malloy has been involved in art and telecommunications for many years. In

addition to working on her own narrative databases and online art she

works part-time as an editor for Leonardo Electronic News, as an International

Co-Editor for Leonardo and as a writer for the Multimedia section of MicroTimes.

She also hosts the arts conference on the WELL and co-hosts the Telluride Conference

on the WELL with Howard Rheingold and Matisse Enzor.

Her own online work was featured as part of Women Networking at SIGGRAPH '93 and she

recently returned from Telluride Colorado where she was a visiting artist at the Deep Creek

School, working with graduate (MFA) students in art and telecommunications. At

Telluride she also helped set up "Tele-Community", a community-based computer network.
