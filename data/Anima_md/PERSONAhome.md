## Community/Context

[Vancouver, B.C, Canada](MAT/Perspectives/Vancouver.html)

Where are communities online? Although cyberspace tends to be non-geographical,

we've included a piece on , where we are located.

[a set](http://www.cs.washington.edu/research/community-networks/)

[of surveys from over 30 community networks](http://www.cs.washington.edu/research/community-networks/)

As part of an ongoing project on community networks, are now available.

Community (computer) Networks are becoming increasingly important as

people in communities all over the world begin developing them to

address needs in their own community.  Developers of community

networks need to be able to find information and documents as well as

people with whom they can discuss issues and concerns.  These surveys

are intended to help develop a "community memory" for community

network developers.
