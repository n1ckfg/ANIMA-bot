# [NEXUS - Network Projects by Artists](ANIMAhome.html)

#### Investigation of aesthetic and critical issues on the interface between art and technology...

NEXUS supports exploratory projects by artists, theorists, and researchers in avante-garde and

leading edge developments in the use of telecommunications as both content and medium of creativity.

These projects vary in scope and intent, from the literary and critical to the expressive and visual.

## [Making Art Online](NEXUS/makingArt.html)

**by** [**Judy Malloy**](MAT/People/aboutJudy.html)

MAKING ART ONLINE is a database of artists' words about using data

transmission systems.  Statements conveying information and ideas about

making art online are interlinked by subject. The work is collaboratively written by artists using

their own words to describe their projects.

## [Towards a Necessarily Furious Philosophy of the Discrete](NEXUS/Oliver/one.html)

**by** [**Oliver Hockenhull**](MAT/People/aboutOliver.html)

A hopscotch of critical theory, new media research, and the interrogation of the aesthetics of the digital inscription played upon

the canvas of language, power, and historical/political recollection

## [Imag(in)ing Networks](NEXUS/netwrk.html)

[Hank Bull](MAT/People/aboutHank.html) and the Western Front organised this telematic art event to celebrate art's Birthday,

January 19th, 1994 by creating an interactive model of the network in the collective imagination.

```
How do you imagine the network?

                    Is it a spiral, a nerve, a mathematical phrase
                    molecular, monocular, rhythmic or knot
                    tree form or free form or
                    what have you got?
```
