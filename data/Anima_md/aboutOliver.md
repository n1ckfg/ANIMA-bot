## Oliver Hockenhull

<!-- 🖼️❌ Image not available. Please use `PdfPipelineOptions(generate_picture_images=True)` -->

Oliver Hockenhull's father flew bombing missions over Dresden.  His mother is an

abstract expressionist.  Oliver is a writer, teacher, filmmaker, film theorist,

and communications consultant. He is currently directing a documentary on Aldous

Huxley.

He is also [developing a software package](../../anima/NEXUS/Oliver/mediabasediscussion.html) for non-linear editing systems and

broadcast interactivity based on formal, aesthetic principals of audio-visual

language.

The project " [The Majestic Violence of Light](../../NEXUS/Oliver/Syntacticaltools.html) " that will demonstrate the software

is seeking assistance.

Oliver has been commisioned to develop an artwork for this project called ["Towards the Necessarily Furious Philosophy of the Discrete"](../../NEXUS/Oliver/one.html)

"A hopscotch of critical theory, new media research, and the interrogation of

the aesthetics of the digital inscription played upon the canvas of language,

power, and historical/political recollection."

oliver@cisr.bc.ca
