Her place was a	mess.  She never bothered putting anything away and kept the most useless	pieces of junk, the most inconsequential flyer and pamphlet from the most	ridiculous museum or flea bit hotel that we had stayed at during one of our	journey's outside the city.  This wasn't done for nostalgic reasons- it was	done because she subconsciously desired to have [everything](red_330.html) , own everything, collect	everything.  Having come from what she felt was a deprived background the	world owed her. I owed her.
