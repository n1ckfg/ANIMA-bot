**AboutDerivative Poetry**

Derivative poetry is aconcept I created with my poems that takes the first word of eachline and uses it to create a new poem. The last line stays intact. Try it yourself. Take the derivative of [*EnergyMatters*](28aApr97.htm) . Thatwould be the second derivative of [*Energy for Life.*](28Apr97.htm)
