You are welcome too	- to come and examine this steaming imbroglio of constipated history... I	have buried my arms to my elbows tracing the labyrinthine course. I have	used all my fingers to follow each convolution, each curvature from the	disconnected beginning to its shitting orifice of a present... Come..Breathe	this mess...Rub your hand in discovery along its cord of blood and	feces... Announce this confusion, this: a pillar of burning light...(Laughs,	holds up the length of intestine) a spine of balanced god dancing in bliss	upon all our charred bodies, an example of immanence and reconciliation -	wish of annihilation and return...
