**Experience the Future Affecting the Past**

We don't need tocross an event horizon on the way to a [blackhole](14Apr97.htm) toexperience the future affecting the past. We can experience it indreams.

When you'redreaming and someone starts to disturb you, or a sound invadesyour dreams from outside, have you ever incorporated the externalevent into your dream? This is quite common for me. Not only isit incorporated, but a whole build-up and preparation is made tointroduce and explain the disturbance.

The first time Iexperienced this was as a child of about nine years. In my dream,I was in the woods and a bear came along and gave me a big bearhug and shook me. It didn't just happen all of a sudden; it wasthe result of a specific progression of events in the dream. Iwoke up to find my mother trying to wake me for school. When Idescribed the dream, she revealed the hug and shaking were fromher.

Since then Ihave often incorporated sounds into a very plausible rationale inmy dream, only to wake and find them still occurring outside.Somehow in my dream, I was able to 'live' the lead-up to an eventthat was only [triggered](04Aug91.htm) in the 'future.'

Perhaps thishappens to you too. By the way, later in my twenties, I did wakeup to a bear sniffing at my tent in the woods. I shouted at itand told it to go away. It did.
