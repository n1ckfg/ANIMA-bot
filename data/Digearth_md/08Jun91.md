**TimeCometh**

Humor is an

Axis of shift

From timelessness

To time here.

It is preferred

For the grasping

Ride!

From time now,

To time then,

The tunnel

Widens -

The net appears...

The grasp is made

From time now,

To time then.

The world of

Need: the world of

Then.

I tear away

To write this

Pen.
