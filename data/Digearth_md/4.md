**WHAT ISNOT** [**GIVEN**](4A.HTML)

Nomadic architecture is abouteverything that has been given. It is about thepassing on of language, culture, rites and rituals and a deeply rooted valuesystem. From its inception this work has been a dedication to the women in mycommunity and especially to my mother. I know that it is the women in thecommunity that have passed on so much with strength and courage.
