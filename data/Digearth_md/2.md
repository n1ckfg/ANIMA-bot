The workarises out of the enquiry of self and questions of identity,displacement, negotiating multiple spaces and living the contradictions ofgrowing up in a western world within a progressive Indian community yet deeplyrooted in traditional value systems.

The work which emerges from the enquiry of self, movesaway from the personaland experiential to the historical, geopolitical and social terrain.
