ARTIST

The technological juggernaut is an extension of our hands and minds.

[Lucinda Atwood](agitp/pill/1.htm) - Imagine a drug of electronic immediacy.  The NEED for

communication, the desire to be plugged in.  Who is pushing and why.

[Antonia Hirsch](antonia/utopia3.html) - Heimweh. Ocean of desire spawning

sensual movement.

[Oliver Hockenhull](klik.htm) - Mouth full of tongue and consistent wander.

Philosophical hypertext rants, agit-prop, and postcards of the metaphysical self.   Extensive.

[Mike MacDonald](macd.htm) - Silent rendering of patient watching.  Butterflies fluttering.

[David Nandi Odhiambo and Terence Anthony](phunked/pu1.html) - Black Urban poetry.  Images isolated,

painted, made uncanny.  A tripping click and the upfront life of the working artists.

[K. D. Thornton](eaaco/index.html) - Consumate use of the Internet as an artistic exploration, discovery and mapping.

A new Universe reflecting on the Ancient, the mundane and the digital.  One of the most

conclusive speakings about the medium, through the medium, on the net.

[Zainub Verjee](zainub/1.HTM) -  A webbed sound journal of another home and the close listening  to the maternal.

We are exploring

how to make gestures that fulfill the forms of our histories and desires.
