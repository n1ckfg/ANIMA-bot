Part of me loves	you, part of me pities you, part of me fears you, part of me hates you ,	most of me is now utterly humoured by it all...and is incited to knowledge	by the sinuous roads of what was once the torturous necessity of my/our	love...again...devotion...again our mutual lies and vacant bliss,	expended as if immortal. You know how much your taste remains with	me. (I feel nauseous when I see you on the street.)
