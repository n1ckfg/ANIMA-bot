Between the silence and the listening is about oral culture.  The passing down of history from mouth to mouth. it is also about the time between the telling of one story and the next story. This is the time of silence and for imagination.

my concerns for the sound piece for nomadic architecture come from oral culture and being aware of the aural environment.

Since last September I have been keeping a sound journal to articulate or find away to notate the many possible sounds structures that I hear in my head for the work. This has become very necessary especially as I do not have a background in music. The journal notes I make are very simple.
