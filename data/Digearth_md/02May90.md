**Non-ModelCommunication**

Anything is Everything it is and Everything it isn't.

By focusing so much attention onthe concept of communicating about a model, our culture hassegregated non-model communication and labeled it art. Non-modelcommunication needs to be integrated back into communicationabout models in order to restore humanity to our culture.

Non-model communication ismodel-free communication and needs no models to convey itsmessage. Model-free expression stems from "emotionalthinking" while reception of such expression is interpretedby emotional experience.

Examples of model-freecommunication include sighs, laughter, tone-poems; gestures,graphic strokes which are "shadows" of gestures,colours, temperatures, touches.

Any thinking has an emotionalcomponent that needs no model to be communicated.
